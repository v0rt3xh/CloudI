**Definition of Altocumulus**: 
White or grey, or both white and grey, patch, sheet or layer of cloud, 
generally with shading, composed of laminae, rounded masses, rolls, etc., 
which are sometimes partly fibrous or diffuse and which may or may not be merged.

**Defintion of Cirrocumulus**: 
Thin, white patch, sheet or layer of cloud without shading, 
composed of very small elements in the form of grains, ripples, etc., 
merged or separate, and more or less regularly arranged.
